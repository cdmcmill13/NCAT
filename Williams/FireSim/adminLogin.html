Group Identifier:



Enter a name to identify the group of people participating in this firewall simulation.

Participants using the firewall simulator will need to enter this group identifier.

The group identifier allows simultaneous use of the simulator by separate groups.
Example identifiers might be COMP476 or NCATF16.

The instructor or administrator must enter the group identifier before any of the participants can log in. Be sure to inform the participants of the group identifier.
